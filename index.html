About my Self

My name is Barr. Sonny Ewule. I am from Oduoha in Emohua LGA. I am a legal
practitioner and I have been practicing law in Rivers State for 30years now, having been called to Bar in 1992. I believe Emohua needs proper, capable, mature and experienced representation. We must ensure that Emohua's representation at the Assembly continues to be PDP. My interest in the race is borne out of a desire to continue to render service to my people in this capacity as I have always done, to ensure that the voice of Emohua is heard and heard clearly. We have a duty to our generation and those unborn to make sure we leave Emohua better than we met it. As a legislator, I will do my bit to initiate bills with direct bearing on the lives of my people in particular, and Rivers people in general. I will initiate and support policies capable of providing a better standard of life for Emohua indigenes and the state at large. My priority will be to look at issues with direct impact on the welfare and socioeconomic status of my people. Addressing issues of poor formal education and increasing rate of unemployment in my constituency, are foremost on my list of priorities concerning the welfare of my people. I would introduce a bill for the establishment of vocational institutes to reduce unemployment and give our youths some sense of belonging. I will partner with the executive arm to see how schools in Emohua could be upgraded to ensure a better learning environment with greater potential for capacity building. I believe I have what it takes to represent my people. I am ready, prepared and able to deliver on all my promises.
